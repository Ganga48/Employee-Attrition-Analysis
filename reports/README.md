# Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

Employee Attrition Analysis is a Business Intelligence project that aims to identify the major factors influencing employee turnover. The project combines **Python** for data preprocessing and exploratory data analysis (EDA), **SQL** for business-oriented analysis, and **Tableau** for interactive dashboard development.

The objective is to transform raw HR employee data into meaningful insights that help organizations improve employee retention and make informed workforce decisions. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition patterns.
- Clean and preprocess HR employee data.
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Answer business questions using SQL.
- Develop an interactive Tableau dashboard.
- Generate insights to support HR decision-making.

## Key Insights

- The organization has an attrition rate of approximately **16.12%**.
- Research & Development department records the highest employee exits.
- Employees working overtime have higher attrition.
- Frequent business travel is associated with higher employee turnover.
- Most employees belong to the 28–38 age group.
- Job satisfaction and work-life balance significantly influence retention. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## Business Recommendations

- Improve work-life balance.
- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Focus retention strategies on high-risk departments.
- Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
- Strengthen employee engagement programs.
